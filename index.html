Each case has a victim and 4 witnesses. All witnesses provide you statements regarding their whereabouts at the time when the crime occurred. One of the witnesses is the culprit and is lying, therefore their statement cannot be true. You must inspect the crime scene and the alibi scenes to find evidence that contradicts this witness' statement. The witness with the contradictory statement is the culprit. Beware of decoy clues!
